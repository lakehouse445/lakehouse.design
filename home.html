Moderator’s Deputies Program Discontinued

It is with a heavy heart that we announce the discontinuation of the Moderator’s Deputies program. Following a formal cease and desist from the Homigrad community, we have decided to comply fully with their request to cease operations immediately.

While this program was created with the intention of supporting the community, fostering fairness, and providing an opportunity for aspiring moderators, we respect the wishes of the Homigrad team and community members who believe this initiative was not in alignment with their vision.

We sincerely thank all those who supported this effort and contributed their time and energy to making it what it was. Your dedication and enthusiasm were invaluable, and we hope that you continue to thrive as part of the Homigrad community.

If you have any questions or need further clarification, please reach out via Discord or other available channels.
